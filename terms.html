Program name: Kreck Family Assistant

Program Description

The Kreck Family Assistant is a private home-assistant messaging program that sends reminders, notifications, alerts, and conversational replies via SMS/MMS to family members who have provided their consent to participate.

How to Opt In

Participation is limited to family members who have given prior express consent to receive messages from this program.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the assistant and the notifications you have set up.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Help

For help, reply HELP at any time, or contact us at support@caseykreck.com.

Opt Out

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP at any time. After you send STOP, you will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent unless you opt back in.

Privacy

Your information is handled according to our Privacy Policy. We do not share your mobile information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@caseykreck.com.
