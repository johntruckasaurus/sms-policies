Program Description

The Kreck Family Assistant is a private home-assistant messaging program that sends reminders, notifications, alerts, and conversational replies via SMS/MMS.

How to Opt In

Participation is limited to family members who have given prior express consent to receive messages from this program.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the assistant and the notifications you have set up.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Help

For help, reply HELP at any time, or contact us at kreckranch@gmail.com.

Opt Out

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP at any time. After you send STOP, you will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Privacy

Your information is handled according to our Privacy Policy at privacy.html. We do not share your mobile information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at kreckranch@gmail.com.
