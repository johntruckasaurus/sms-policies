Privacy Policy

Kreck Family Assistant

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Kreck Family Assistant ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our SMS/MMS text messaging service.

Information We Collect

We collect the mobile phone numbers of family members who have agreed to receive messages, and the content of messages sent to and from the service.

How We Use Information

We use this information solely to operate the personal home-assistant messaging service: to send reminders, notifications, and alerts, and to respond to messages from authorized family members.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information is only processed by our messaging provider (Twilio) as needed to deliver messages.

Data Retention

We retain message data only as long as necessary to operate the service. You may request deletion of your information at any time.

Opt-Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at kreckranch@gmail.com.
